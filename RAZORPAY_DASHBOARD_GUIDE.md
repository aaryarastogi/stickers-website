# Razorpay Dashboard - Enable Domestic Payments Guide

## Where to Find Payment Settings

The Razorpay dashboard interface may vary, but here are the common locations:

### Option 1: Settings → Account & Settings
1. Log in to https://dashboard.razorpay.com
2. Click on **Settings** (gear icon) in the left sidebar
3. Look for:
   - **Account & Settings**
   - **Payment Settings**
   - **Payment Methods**
   - **Payment Configuration**

### Option 2: Settings → Payment Methods
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Look for **Payment Methods** or **Payment Options**
3. Check for **Domestic Cards** toggle/checkbox

### Option 3: Account Settings
1. Click on your **Account Name/Profile** (top right)
2. Go to **Account Settings**
3. Look for **Payment Methods** or **Payment Configuration**

### Option 4: Products → Payment Gateway
1. Go to **Products** in the sidebar
2. Click on **Payment Gateway**
3. Look for payment method settings

## If You Can't Find the Option

### For Test Accounts:
Test accounts sometimes have limited settings. Try these:

1. **Check Account Activation:**
   - Go to **Settings** → **Account**
   - Ensure your account is fully activated
   - Complete any pending verification

2. **Contact Razorpay Support:**
   - Email: support@razorpay.com
   - Or use the chat support in dashboard
   - Mention: "Need to enable domestic card payments for test account"
   - Provide your Key ID: `rzp_test_RfA1p0DJC2RGk4`

3. **Check Account Type:**
   - Some test accounts are restricted
   - You might need to create a new test account
   - Or upgrade to a business account
